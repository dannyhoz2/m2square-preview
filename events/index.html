01 — WHAT WE PRODUCE

Built for milestones, not menus.

We're not a venue, a caterer, or a DJ. We're the producer who hires them, briefs them, and makes sure they all show up doing the right thing on the right day.

Bar & Bat Mitzvahs

From the Havdalah candle to the grand entrance to the after-party — every tradition honored, every kid on the dance floor, every parent breathing. Bilingual production (English & Hebrew).

Weddings

Multi-day, multi-cultural, or just intimate and beautiful. We handle vendor curation, ceremony flow, and reception logistics so you actually get to be present.

Milestone Birthdays & Anniversaries

50ths, 60ths, big anniversaries, vow renewals. Designed to feel personal first, polished second.

Family & Lifecycle Celebrations

Brises, baby namings, retirement parties, end-of-life remembrances. The moments that matter, treated with the care they deserve.

From a recent event

For Ellie's bat mitzvah we built a custom guest mobile app — every guest scanned a QR code at their table to find their seat, browse the menu, follow the timeline, and share photos in real time. That's the level of production we bring.

02 — HOW IT WORKS

Embedded with you, from vision to last guest out.

Vision

What do you want guests to feel?

Before we touch logistics, we figure out the heart of the event — the moments that have to land, the family stories that have to show up, the feeling at midnight.

Design & Plan

Every vendor, every minute, documented.

Vendor curation, venue layout, run-of-show, dietary tracking, transport, signage. Every decision lives in a private dashboard you can check anytime.

The Day

First in, last out — so you can be present.

We're on-site from vendor load-in to teardown. Senior operator running the floor. Your only job that day is to show up and be in it.

03 — RECENT WORK

A few moments we made happen.

Tablescapes, ceremonies, dance floors, dessert moments, late-night second courses — every detail considered, every vendor in sync.

04 — WHO YOU'LL WORK WITH

Meet Yakira.

Yakira Hoz has spent a decade producing events — from 200-person corporate offsites to intimate family celebrations. She works at the intersection of logistics and human connection: the unglamorous middle that makes the rest possible.

Based in the SF Bay Area and bilingual (English & Hebrew), she partners with families across the U.S. and Israel on the events that matter most. Every engagement is led by Yakira herself — there is no junior bench, and there are no hand-offs.

"I treat your event like it's my own family's — because for those weeks, it is."
