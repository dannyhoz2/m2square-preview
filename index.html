02 - HOW WE WORK

Embedded, not outsourced.

  1. Step 1

    Discover

    A 30-minute intro call. We pin down scope, timeline, and pricing before anyone signs anything. No fishing expeditions.

  2. Step 2

    Embed

    Shared Slack channel, weekly standups, your tools. Treat us like internal team - because for those weeks, we are.

  3. Step 3

    Deliver

    You get the receipts: budget reconciled, vendor list, retro doc. Hand-off is documented so your team can run with it.

03 - WHO YOU'LL WORK WITH

Meet Yakira.

Yakira Hoz has spent a decade running operations and producing events for fast-moving teams. She works at the intersection of logistics, collaboration, and human connection - the unglamorous middle that makes the rest possible.

Based in the San Francisco Bay Area, she partners with U.S. and Israeli startups: building U.S. footprints, designing offsites that people actually talk about, and standing up the operating cadence a growing team needs.
